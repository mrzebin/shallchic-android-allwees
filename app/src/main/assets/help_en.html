  • The size, color or variation you selected is priced differently. In some cases, the price of the item may vary depending on your choice of size, color, or variation.
  • The same item is being sold by multiple stores. Stores set their own prices for each item, so it's possible for different stores to sell the same product at different prices.

Why was I charged twice for my order?

In some cases, our payment processor may have trouble capturing your payment on its first attempt. Our system will then re-try the capture. This means that you may see a duplicate charge on your statement that is authorized but not captured.

Depending on your bankâ€™s policies, you may see this duplicate/pending charge credited or completely removed from your statement within the next 14 days, but it could take longer. Please contact your bank to verify how long it may take for the charge to be removed.

When will I be charged for my order?

Depending on which payment method you use, it may take a week or two for our system to process your payment and ship your order.

However, you may see an authorization on your account once your order is placed. If you decide to cancel your order within 8 hours, your bank will either credit or remove the authorization from your statement.

Why is my payment being declined?

Your payment may be declined for one of the following reasons:

  • Your card is expired.
  • You have insufficient funds.
  • Your card was reported as stolen or frozen.
  • If you are using a debit card, it may not have a CVV and/or cannot be billed as a credit card. We recommend contacting your bank or financial institution to resolve your payment issues.
